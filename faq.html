Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Amber Fashion?+

Amber Fashion is an online fashion brand specializing in trendy, premium-quality T-shirts designed for comfort, style, and individuality.

2. How do I place an order?+

Simply browse our products, click “Add to Cart,” and proceed to checkout. You can securely complete your purchase in just a few steps.

3. What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept all major payment methods including UPI, debit/credit cards, and net banking. COD (Cash on Delivery) is available in select regions.

4. How long will my order take to arrive?+

Orders are typically delivered within 5–7 business days depending on your location.

5. Can I return or exchange a product?+

Yes, you can request a return or exchange within 7 days of delivery, provided the item is unused and in original condition.

6. How do I contact customer support?+

You can reach us anytime at amber.business2025@gmail.com or via the Contact Us page on our website.
